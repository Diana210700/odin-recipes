Vegetarian Pancakes

Description

They are fluffy, they are delicious, they are sweet. They are laterally the best. But everyone has their own opinion, so like, duh. Try them out and decide for yourself.

Ingredients

Plant Milk
1 cup
I use oat milk, but feel free to choose a different one. But it should be plant-based.
Flour
1 cup
We kinda doing this 1:1 ratio here.
Apple vinegar
1/2 tbs
That's why we "can't just use milk instead".
Soda or Leaven
1 tsp
Mixed with vinegar will make you pancakes fluffy.
Butter or Vegetable Oil
2 biiiig spoons
So it won't stick to the pan. You can use vegetable oil instead.
Cinnamon
For your taste
You don't have to use it, but it will definitely make you pancakes better.

Steps

  1. Sift The Flour
    • Please, do it.

  2. Add Vinegar to Milk
    • Pretty easy step, right?

  3. Add Milk-Vinegar Mixture to the Flour
    • Add milk slowly and mix the whole time. I use a mixer, but you can just use a whisker or a fork (not a good idea thou).

  4. Keep Mixing
    • You need a smooth mix.

  5. Add Butter
    • Keep on mixing!

  6. Add Soda (Leaven)
    • Still mixing, but not that fast.

  7. Add Cinnamon
    • Optional.

  8. Okay, thats enough mixing
    • Let it rest until you see air bubbles on top the mix.

  9. Cook Them
    • No oil or butter would be needed, so just use a ladle or whatever and pour your mixture on the pan. When you see that one side became kinda triggering for trypophobes that's time to flip them over.

  10. Eat what you made!
    • That's what food is for, innit?
