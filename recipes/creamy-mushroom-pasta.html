Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Description

You can imagine how easy is it cook. Well... You have an electric coffee grinder, right?

Ingredients

Heavy Cream
500ml
Anything with 20% and higher will be good.
Dried Mushrooms
1 pack
Yeah, that's what we need a grinder for.
Mushrooms
400g
You can use any mushrooms, or don't use them at all.
Pasta
400g
A whole pack basically.

Steps

  1. Grind The Mushrooms
    • Okay, we need to adress it. All those mushroom sold in the supermarkets are actually not that mushroomy, espetially those Paris mushrooms. So we need to grind some mushrooms for better mushrom taste for our sause.

  2. Cook The Pasta
    • Do it according to the package instructions, but make water extra salty. (Barilla playlist on Spotify is fire).

  3. Fry The Musrooms
    • Don't use oil. That will make give them more nutty and mushroomy taste.

  4. Make the sauce
    • Pour heavy cream on the pan, add a 2 tsp of mushroom powder, you can add a little bit of water and spices if you wish.

  5. Add Pasta
    • Add pasta to the sauce anf mix them together.

  6. Bon Appetit!
    • Pasta is French after all.
